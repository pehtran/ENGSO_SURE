Welcome to the SURE Search Tool, an interactive platform developed under the Erasmus+ Sport for Unity, Resilience and Empowerment (SURE) project (2024–2026). This tool is designed to help grassroots sports organisations assess and strengthen their capacity across key operational areas. Based on extensive academic research and practical guidelines compiled by a global consortium of 11 partners across 13 countries, the evaluation will measure your organisation's current performance in fostering inclusivity for vulnerable groups, building community partnerships, and preparing for major crises like energy shortages, displacement, or pandemics. Use this assessment to identify structural gaps, improve community access, and ensure your club remains resilient through challenging times.
